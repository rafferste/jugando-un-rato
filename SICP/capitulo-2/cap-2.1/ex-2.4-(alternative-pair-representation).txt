------------------------------- Exercise 2.4 -----------------------------------   
Here is an alternative procedural representation of pairs. For this 
representation, verify that (car (cons x y)) yields x for any objects x and y.

(define (cons x y)
  (lambda (m) (m x y)))

(define (car z)
  (z (lambda (p q) p)))

What is the corresponding definition of cdr? (Hint: To verify that this works, 
make use of the substitution model of section 1.1.5.)

(define (cdr z)
  (z (lambda (p q) q)))

(define x (cons 2 5))

Substitution model

(car x)

(car (cons 2 5))

(car (lambda (m) (m 2 5)))

((lambda (m) (m 2 5)) (lambda (p q) p))

((lambda (p q) p) 2 5)

2
